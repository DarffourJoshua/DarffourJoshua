### 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a **Full Stack Developer** with 3+ years of experience building production-grade web applications and AI-powered platforms. I graduated from **All Nations University** with a **BSc in Computer Science (2nd Class Upper)** and have since shipped real products to real users across fintech, edtech, and SaaS.

I build across the full stack — React, Next.js, TypeScript on the frontend; Node.js, PostgreSQL, GraphQL, and REST APIs on the backend — and I integrate AI into products using LangChain, GroqAI, and LLM tooling. I work best in fast-moving teams where every decision matters and shipping something means something.
